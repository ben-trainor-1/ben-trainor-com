Paper Watch

This is my solo project that I publish to any and all streaming platforms. My style is a unique combination of acoustic instruments, catchy melodies, rock driven energy, and late romantic/20th century harmony. Though 99% instrumental, many find my music to be great study ambience as well as distinct enough to give their full attention to it.

Original scores

I have had the pleasure of working on a handful of scores, both for film and theater (more so the latter). I've written an intimate and emotional score for Union University's production of The Count of Monte Cristo, a minimalist but poignant piano score for Hub City Theatre Company's production of Anges of God, and I have more projects in the works.
