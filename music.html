Original scores

I have had the pleasure of working on a handful of scores, both for film and theater (more so the latter). I've written an intimate and emotional score for Union University's production of The Count of Monte Cristo, a minimalist but poignant piano score for Hub City Theatre Company's production of Anges of God, and I have more projects in the works. Take a listen!
