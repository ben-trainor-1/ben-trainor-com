About me

Hey there! My name is Ben Trainor, and I'm passionate about too many things. I love photography, playing guitar, recording and mixing music, and writing scores. Hit me up if you're working on a short film, video game, or want some portraits taken!

What I do

Music

Composer for solo project Paper Watch and freelance composer for short film The Claw (2020) and stage production The Count of Monte Cristo (2022).

Sound

Live audio engineer for Barefoots Joe Coffee Roasters (2019-2022), recording and mixing engineer for That Band single, Lydia, and sound designer for The Claw (2020) and The Count of Monte Cristo (2022).

Visual

Guitar

Experienced guitarist trained in contemporary and classical traditions since 2008, open for background music for weddings or recording sessions.
