Photography

My philosophy

I love photographing candid moments and the beauty I find around me. My editing style is minimal, and I try to bring out what's there in the photo rather than force the picture to look a certain way.

Locations

Based in Jackson, TN, open to shooting in the West/Middle Tennessee Area. For locations 1 or more hours away, I factor in gas and $10/hour driven.
