Photography

I love photographing candid moments and the beauty I find around me. My editing style is minimal, and I try to bring out what's there in the photo rather than force the picture to look a certain way. I am flexible on dim or bright aesthetics and have a wider 28mm and a medium zoom 50mm lens which gives me some versatility.
